Why help change the climate action?

"Climate change, urgent global challenge, results from human activities, causing shifts in temperature, precipitation, and atmospheric conditions."

"Climate change impacts ecosystems, weather patterns, sea levels, and societies, leading to environmental degradation, extreme weather, loss of biodiversity, and threats to food security. Collective action is essential for sustainability."

Why is this a problem?

"As temperatures climb, so do the stakes. With each degree of warming, we witness a cascade of impacts: more frequent extreme weather events, like hurricanes and wildfires, and a surge in sea levels, imperiling coastal communities. The statistics paint a dire picture: record-breaking heatwaves, melting polar ice caps, and a ticking clock on our planet's future. Urgent action is not just a choice; it's a necessity for our survival."
